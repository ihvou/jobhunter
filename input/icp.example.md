# ICP

Describe the leads you want the agent to research.

Example:

- Founders or product leaders at B2B SaaS companies.
- Company is actively adopting AI agents, Claude Code, Codex, or workflow automation.
- Team size: 10-200.
- Geography: US, UK, EU.
- Good signal: hiring for AI/product roles, shipping AI workflow features, writing about agentic automation.
- Avoid: agencies, crypto trading, consumer mobile games, generic AI content farms.

# Outreach Rules

- Do not send messages automatically.
- Use public professional contact surfaces only.
- Prefer LinkedIn profile URLs as manual review links, not as scraped data.
- Draft short, specific pitches for manual copy/paste only.
