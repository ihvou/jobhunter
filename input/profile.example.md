# Candidate Profile

Copy this file to `input/profile.local.md` and replace it with a concise description of the jobs
you want. This is the primary input for scoring and source discovery.

Recommended sections:

- Target roles
- Role goals
- Strengths and evidence
- Location/timezone constraints
- Salary floor
- Dealbreakers

Example:

Product manager. Product lead. Product owner. Head of product. Product builder.
Product engineer. I want roles where I create product prototypes, MVPs, or implement
new features in existing products with Claude Code, Codex, or related AI tooling.

Another good fit is implementing AI-based features or optimizing business processes
through AI automation.

Strengths: product and feature discovery, product analytics, multi-stakeholder
environments, fast prototyping, and turning vague business problems into shipped
product increments.

Avoid: internships, junior-only roles, US-only roles, gambling, adult, defense,
and jobs that require security clearance.
