# Jobhunter Source Discovery

Find sources that can produce many relevant jobs with low operational risk.

Preference order:

1. Job aggregators and searchable boards with RSS/API/static listings.
2. Public ATS boards such as Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workable, and Recruitee.
3. High-signal niche communities, newsletters, conference boards, and founder/operator job boards.
4. Email alerts for valuable sites that should not be scraped.
5. Company career pages only as curated exceptions.

Use OpenClaw web tools such as `web_search`, `web_fetch`, `browser`, `firecrawl`, or `exa` when available. Validate source candidates before recommending them.
